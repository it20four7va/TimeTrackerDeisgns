Do you agree with our week-withhold policy?

We have a week withhold policy wherein we withhold a total of 200% of your weekly pay starting on the 1st week and staggered up until the 3rd week (1st week - 100% withhold, 2nd & 3rd week - 50% withhold). We are withholding the salary for the security & guarantee of the Client and the Company and this amount is intact and will be released as soon as you end with a Client and just as long as you are in good standing. Pls note that there's a withhold in pay every time there would be an increase in the weekly contracted hours. The payroll cut off we follow is Mon-Sun EST. Pay is sent via Transferwise weekly, every Wednesday, on or before 12mn EST. We will also be transitioning to an every-other-week pay and we will just let you know in advance once this will take effect.
