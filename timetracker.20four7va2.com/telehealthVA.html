WHAT IS A TELEHEALTH
VIRTUAL ASSISTANT?

A Telehealth Virtual Assistant (VA) is a remote worker who provides administrative support, customer support, creative support, or technical support services to healthcare practices or healthcare facilities.

Outsourcing tasks to a Telehealth VA allows you to focus on providing better care to your clients by delegating office tasks that can be done remotely to a trained, reliable, and trustworthy virtual worker
